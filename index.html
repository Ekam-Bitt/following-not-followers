    How to use it

    1. Download your Instagram information in JSON format.
    2. Locate the followers and following files in the unzipped export.
    3. Upload both files here and compare them instantly.

    Why it is private

    • Your files stay in the browser and are never sent to a backend.
    • You do not need to log in with your Instagram account.
    • You can save the page and run it locally with a basic static server.

    What the tool checks

    The app compares usernames in your exported following list against usernames in your followers list, then shows the accounts that appear only in following.
